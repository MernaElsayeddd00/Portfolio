

This project uses a **Google Service Account** for secure, non-interactive access to Google Sheets and Drive.

### Steps to Set Up Credentials:

1. Go to the [Google Cloud Console](https://console.cloud.google.com/).
2. Create/select a project and enable:
   - **Google Sheets API**
   - **Google Drive API**
3. Navigate to **IAM & Admin > Service Accounts**.
4. Create a new Service Account, assign it the **Editor** role.
5. Go to **Keys > Add Key > JSON** and download the file.
6. Rename the downloaded file to `credentials.json` and place it in the project root.

### Important: Grant Access to Sheets

The service account can only access sheets that are **explicitly shared** with it:

1. Open each of the **4 source Google Sheets** (Sales, Financials, Account Management, Call Center).
2. Click **Share** and paste the `client_email` address from your `credentials.json`.
3. Give it **Editor** access.
4. Do the same for the **target spreadsheet** (the one containing the "Combined_ETL" tab).

> **Note**: Sharing with the service account’s email is required for both reading from source sheets and writing to the combined sheet.
